Description of data:

These meteorological data represent approximately 20,000 NCDC stations across
CONUS, (plus a small number of Enviro-Can stations for the Canadian portion
of the Columbia river) gridded to 211687 points at a 1/16 degree spatial resolution, running from 
1 Jan 1915 - 31 Dec 2011 and with the following columns:


<daily precipitation (mm)> <daily maximum temperature (C)> <daily minimum temperature (mm)> <daily mean wind speed (m/s)>
